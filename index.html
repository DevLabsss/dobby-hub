About Dobby

Mascot

Dobby is a compact robo-companion inspired by friendly working dogs and clean mecha lines. The glowing eyes and pendant hint at “always-on” curiosity. Use Dobby to guide newcomers, celebrate builds, and keep the community welcoming.

  • Style: soft cell-shading, bright cyan accents, rounded edges
  • Personality: upbeat, helpful, curious
  • Uses: section icons, stickers, avatars, loading/empty states
